About Me

Hi! I am Zacque (pronounced as Zac, the "que" is silent), originating from Penang but has spend almost my entire life living in the bustling capital city of Malaysia, Kuala Lumpur.

Here are is a quick summary on my career so far:

  • Graduated from APU, with Bachelor's Degree in IT, majoring in Forensic Computing.
  • Worked as Programmer Analyst for a outsourcing powerhouse in Petaling Jaya and was outsourced to a few key takaful/insurance companies in Malaysia, participated in software integration project/deployment.
  • Picked up the knowledge on coding in COBOL on AS/400 mainframe systems, the bread and butter of my job involves maintaining/bug-fixing/enhancing/writing new batch programs and to provide BAU(Business As Usual) support as required.
  • Gained a few years experience, moved on as a Senior Application Developer, working directly in a P&C insurance company in-house application team.

The grass is always greener on the other side as you may say, despite having knowledge on coding in COBOL on AS/400 mainframe systems, which many may consider it as niche and even a valuable skill; it did not provide much in career opportunities and flexibility that i yearn.

I have always been keen on exploring modern web development and to transition myself as a web developer.

Sigma School would be my transition point.
